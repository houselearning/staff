About

Khalid Sal Ameri is the dedicated **Firebase & Database Manager** for HouseLearning. He is responsible for architecting and securing the critical data infrastructure that underpins the entire platform. Leveraging his extensive knowledge in cloud services and data integrity, visible through his contributions on GitHub under the handle `khalid-ameri`, Khalid ensures that all educational content is stored, managed, and retrieved with maximum efficiency and security. His commitment is vital to maintaining the reliability and performance of HouseLearning, guaranteeing a seamless and protected learning experience for the global community.

Khalid specializes in scalable, real-time database solutions and is deeply involved in implementing security policies to protect user and content integrity, ensuring our open-source platform remains trustworthy.
